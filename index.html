WELCOME TO NEO-TOKYO

The year is 2077. Welcome to the most advanced, dangerous, and vibrant city on Earth. Where chrome meets flesh and data flows like water.

CITY DISTRICTS

Ginza Heights

Corporate central where megacorps tower over the city. All chrome and glass, but watch your step in the back alleys.

Akihabara Circuit

Tech heaven and black market central. If it runs on electricity, you can find it here - legally or otherwise.

Shinjuku Slums

Where the city's underbelly thrives. Not for tourists, but if you need something done off the books...

Roppongi Entertainment

HOW TO NOT GET ZEROED

Watch Your Back in Shinjuku
The slums aren't a tourist attraction. Travel in groups and avoid eye contact with gangers.
Don't Flash Your Chrome
Expensive cyberware makes you a target. Keep high-end mods concealed when possible.
Corpo Zones Have Different Rules
Corporate security has shoot-to-kill authorization. Don't trespass in Ginza Heights.
Data Hygiene is Critical
Use ICE (Intrusion Countermeasures) on your personal devices. Netrunners are always scanning for vulnerabilities.
Know Your Food Sources
Stick to licensed food stalls. Unregulated synth-meat can have... unexpected side effects.
Credits Over Cash
Digital transactions are safer than physical currency, which attracts unwanted attention.
